About us

Our mission

The ETHS Rocketry is a student organization at Evanston Township High School. We aim to gain hands-on experience with the fundamentals of rocketry — from airframe design and propulsion to avionics and recovery systems.

Our goal is to prepare members for collegiate rocketry teams and give students a place to build real engineering skills outside the classroom.

Hands-on rocketry
Build and fly real rockets, not simulations. Every member gets their hands on the hardware.
Real engineering skills
Avionics, control systems, CAD, simulation — skills that carry into college and beyond.
National competitions
Competing in ARC and R4S gives us measurable targets and deadlines that keep us focused.
